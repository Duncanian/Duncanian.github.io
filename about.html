About Us.

Hello-Books is one of the finest libraries in the world. It was founded on a simple belief....Brilliance is evenly distributed but opportunity is not. As a result, the goal of the founders was to provide knowledge and opportunity to all... and this we have done since our founding in 1959.
We are passionate about using technology to help us reach out to more people and this tendency of embracing change and innovation has been key to our success. This does not mean that we despise tradition....not at all....in fact our passion for tradition, art and culture has never been  stronger.... Do you feel inspired to learn more? Feel free to join the conversation online...we would love to hear from you.
